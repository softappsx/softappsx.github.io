Privacy Policy

Our app is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Face Data Collection

Our application collects and processes user-uploaded photos that contain face data to generate AI-enhanced muscular video representations. The face data is extracted and analyzed solely for this purpose.

Purpose of Face Data Usage

The collected face data is used exclusively for generating AI-enhanced videos. No other biometric analysis or facial recognition functionalities are implemented in our app.

Third-Party Sharing and Storage

The uploaded photos and face data are processed using the Pixverse.ai API. The data is stored on Pixverse.ai servers and is not shared with any third parties beyond this service provider.

Data Retention Policy

The uploaded images and generated videos are retained indefinitely on Pixverse.ai servers. Users do not have the option to delete their uploaded photos or generated content. We will not keep your personal data for longer than necessary for the purposes set out in this Statement, unless an extension of the retention period is required or permitted by law.
